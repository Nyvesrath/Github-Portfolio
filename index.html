About Me

Get to know me!

Hey! It's Alexandre Cazier and I'm a Game Developer located in France. I'm passionate about games not just as a player, but also as a creator. My love for gaming inspires my work as a game developer, where I bring imaginative worlds and engaging experiences to life.

I specialize in Gameplay Programming because I believe it is the heart of creating engaging player experiences. While I greatly value and respect other programming areas, such as engine, render, sound, and tool development, my passion lies in designing and implementing mechanics that bring games to life. I focus on crafting intuitive and responsive gameplay systems that make each moment immersive and rewarding for players.

Air Splitter

AirSplitter is a racing game developed in C++ on Unreal Engine, where the only thing you control is your speed by accelerating or decelerating. During the development, I worked on the collision reaction and the physics that results. I also handled the the management of speed through player controls, and after that, I helped with additional features implementation

Galatea, Game Engine and GravityGun

Galatea is a 3D game engine with an editor, developed in C++. This engine was created over five months during my second year, as part of a team of four programmers. For this engine, I focused particularly on implementing reflection and serialization of engine data, enabling its integration into our editor. This made the editor functional and allowed us to save and load data effectively. GravityGun is a game developed in C++ using our Galatea engine. In this game, you play as an unknown test subject tasked with testing a new weapon. This weapon allows you to manipulate gravity by either absorbing the gravity of surrounding objects or transferring your gravity to specific objects. You must navigate a series of challenges that combine skill and puzzle-solving.

Bioluminescent Forest, Tech-art

This project served as an introduction to the role of a tech artist. It showcases a bioluminescent forest that reacts dynamically to player movements and actions, illuminating the foliage and bending the grass in response. The environment also features a seamless day-night cycle, adding depth and realism to the scene, with a strong focus on optimizing effects for performance efficiency.

Undivided

Undivided is a game developed as part of the Brawler 3D project in collaboration with game designers. The core concept revolves around defeating enemies using both your sword and shield together, with each enhancing the other: the upgraded shield knocks enemies farther away, while the upgraded sword deals significantly more damage. Use this gameplay mechanic effectively to climb to the Pantheon of Heroes and secure your place among the legends.
